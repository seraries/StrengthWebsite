Why The Starting Strength Method?

  1. RichardsonProgramming
  2. Why Strength Train?
  3. Why Starting Strength?

The Starting Strength program, developed by Mark Rippetoe, is the fastest way to get as strong as possible as quickly as possible. (Even The New York Times agrees). It has been used by powerlifters, competitive athletes, and everyday people for over 25 years. It consists primarily of just 5 basic lifts: squat, bench, deadlift, press, and power clean. For an intro to barbell training and for videos of these 5 lifts, see the "Video Instructions" page. For more info on starting a training program see the Starting Strength website.
